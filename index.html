Cost borne by retirees

Implementing policies such as increasing the state retirement age or removing the triple lock guarantee would improve pension sustainability, at the cost of current retirees:

  • The removal of the triple lock or increasing the retirement age would decrease the costs of the pension system, making it more resilient in the short term against the UK's demographic changes.
  • However these policies come at a clear trade-off to current retirees, as they would reduce the value of pensions or delay access to income, potentially increasing financial insecurity and lowering living standards among pensioners.
